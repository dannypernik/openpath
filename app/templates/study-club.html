Join the Open Path Study Club

Tutor led. Student empowered. Time well spent.

The saying is true that if you can teach it, you know it. The Open Path Study Club is a unique opportunity for students give and receive support on the SAT and ACT with peers around the country and the world.

Each Study Club session is led by a tutor, and students take turns bringing questions to the group. If another student knows how to arrive at the answer, both students (and any others interested in understanding that question) will join a Zoom breakout room to work that problem while the rest of the group moves onto another student's question.

Once the initial student sufficiently understands the answer, they will rejoin the main Zoom room and be given the chance to explain the solution to the group. This will allow the tutor to confirm that a valid approach has been taken and add any further suggestions.

This novel format allows session time to be used more efficiently while also making tutoring more affordable.
