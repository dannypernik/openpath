About This App

Test Prep Analysis Tools is a Google Workspace Add-on designed to help tutors and administrators efficiently manage, analyze, and update SAT/ACT student data and folders. The app automates the creation of student folders, links Google Sheets and Docs, and provides tools for updating and transferring student performance data.

Functionality

  • Create and organize SAT/ACT student folders in Google Drive
  • Copy and link Google Sheets and Docs templates for each student
  • Transfer and update student performance data between spreadsheets
  • Automate formatting, formulas, and conditional formatting in student and admin sheets
  • Generate and manage revision sheets and answer keys

Why We Request Access to Your Google Data

This app requires access to your Google Drive and Google Sheets to:

  • Create, copy, and organize student folders and files
  • Read and update student and admin spreadsheets
  • Automate data transfer and formatting between Google Sheets
  • Generate and store revision sheets and answer keys as PDFs

We do not share your data with third parties. All data processing is performed within your Google Workspace environment.
